Nudging toward healthy financial behaviors.

Problem: The client loses $40m / year because many of its customers are late on their bills, and aren't sure why

Method: Ethnographic interviews (w/customers & client retail employees), mixed-methods research (collaboration w/ quant team), sacrificial concepting.

Solution: A detailed taxonomy of late payers, including why they're late, nudges they respond best to, and how to find target them in customer database.

Outcome: We designed 3 targeted "nudge-experiments" to test live on client platform with metrics to evaluate success.
